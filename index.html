About Present Continius

Present continius we usually use when we tell about time which happening now. Of course we use present continius in future. For present continius we use helping words: today, now, in future. For present continius need helping verb "ing", for example I am going to school.

When we say about something negative, we add not and go out, for example: I am not catching zombie, He isn't driving auto.

When we asking we put to be in beginning of sentence and go out for example: Do watching TV, Is she looking for new t-shirt.

For usual sentence we have this structure:I/you/He/She/IT/They/We+are/is/am+verb+ing+another part of sentence.

For negative sentence we have this structure:I/you/He/She/IT/They/We+aren't/isn't/am not+verb+ing+another part of sentence.

For asking sentence we have this structure:are/is/am+I/you/He/She/IT/They/We+verb+ing+another part of sentence.
